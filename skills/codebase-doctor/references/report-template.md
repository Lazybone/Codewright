## Notes

- **Do not forget positive observations** -- pure problem lists are demotivating
- **Highlight quick wins** -- helps with prioritization
- **Metrics provide context** -- 5 findings in 10 files != 5 findings in 5000 files
- **List performed fixes** -- transparency about automatic changes
- **Document NEEDS_REVIEW clearly** -- user must know what is still open
